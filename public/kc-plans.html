June 4 – 6, 2026 · Three Budget Plans
Three plans below. Each one includes Dott Boss ★ Miranda's pick and the Bloch Galleries (the HRB founders' art). The difference is mostly where you spend the rest of the time and money.
🎯 Two Lucky Breaks for Your Trip
1. Your PTO day IS a First Friday. June 5 is the first Friday of June 2026, so the Crossroads Arts District comes alive 5–9 PM with 100+ local artists, food trucks, live music. Walkable from Hotel Phillips.

2. Dott Boss is one block from Hotel Phillips. 101 W 11th St — Hotel Phillips is at 106 W 12th St. Across the street, basically. Easy walk for breakfast any morning.
⭐ The HRB Founders Connection
The Bloch Galleries at the Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art — opened 2017, features ~30 Impressionist & Post-Impressionist masterpieces (Monet, Van Gogh, Matisse, Manet) donated by Henry and Marion Bloch. Admission is free. This is what your colleagues meant.

Bonus: There's also an H&R Block Heritage Wall in the lobby of HQ at 13th & Main. Free, open 8 AM–5 PM weekdays — pop in during a work day.
